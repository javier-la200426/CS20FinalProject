Matchmaking Rules

Users are matched based on the following criteria:

1
At least 2 hobbies shared by ALL group members

Every person in your group must share at least 2 hobbies in common. This ensures the AI suggests activities everyone will enjoy!

2
Same state

All users must be in the same state (e.g., MA).

3
At least 1 overlapping availability

Must have at least one matching day AND time slot with all members.

Example

You: hiking, reading, gaming, cooking | Boston, MA

Match 1: hiking, reading, yoga | Cambridge, MA

Match 2: hiking, reading, tennis | Somerville, MA

✓ Valid Group! All members share: hiking, reading

AI will suggest activities involving hiking or reading
